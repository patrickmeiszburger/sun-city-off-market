About This Property

Single-story ease. On the fairway.

Set along the fairways of Sun City Summerlin, this off-market home is a single-story residence that pairs bright, easy living with one of the most coveted settings in the community. The backyard opens directly onto the golf course, with uninterrupted views of the fairway and the mountains beyond.

Just over 2,102 square feet unfolds in an open, light-filled layout with 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, and a spacious great room anchored by a fireplace. Vaulted ceilings, generous windows, and a warm, neutral palette keep the home feeling airy and inviting throughout.

The kitchen sits at the heart of the home, with abundant cabinetry, a center island, and a casual dining nook that looks out to the backyard. It flows naturally into the living and dining spaces, making both everyday meals and larger gatherings effortless.

The owner's suite is a private retreat with its own ensuite bath, while the second bedroom and full bath give guests a comfortable space of their own. A rare three-car garage adds generous room for vehicles, a workshop, or a golf cart.

Step outside and the home truly sets itself apart. A sparkling pool and spa anchor an extended paver patio with multiple lounging and dining areas, a built-in fire feature, and those open views across the golf course toward the horizon.

Built in 1998 and set on an 8,276 square foot lot, this is a rare opportunity to own a turnkey single-story home with a true resort-style backyard in one of Las Vegas's most established golf communities.
